What is a cardiac MRI scan?

MRI scans produce pictures of your body by using a strong magnetic field and radio frequency pulses. A cardiac MRI scan produces images of the heart muscle and valves and is used to assess both its structure and function.

What does a cardiac MRI scan diagnose?

MRI stands for magnetic resonance imaging. A cardiac MRI scan is used to diagnose a wide range of heart conditions, including congenital heart disease, cardiomyopathy and heart valve disease.

Who carries out a cardiac MRI scan?

All our radiographers have the highest expertise and provide the highest quality of care. Our patients are at the heart of everything we do, and we want you to be in control of your health.

How long does a cardiac MRI scan take?

A cardiac MRI scan takes around 90 minutes, the results will be available within 3 working days of your scan and will be shared to your clinical account. A cardiac MRI scan can provide peace of mind and reassurance.
