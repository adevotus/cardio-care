Surgical Services

We have been perfecting ways to perform life-saving heart procedures since completing the first open-heart surgery. We combine world-renowned expertise with leading research to develop new techniques and technology for making heart surgery safer and more effective for you.

Our heart experts specialize in managing intricate cardiac conditions by closely collaborating with a multidisciplinary cardiovascular surgical team and conducting monthly cardiovascular surgical improvement sessions.

They continually assess guidelines and enhance care standards to ensure optimal patient outcomes. This involves refining patient care pathways to provide a seamless experience.

When you choose us, you benefit from:

Leaders in heart surgery:

Cardiocare Clinic cardiologists are world leaders in heart surgeries, including heart transplantation, our specialized heart surgery team has an international reputation for clinical excellence and pioneering research.

Advanced technology:

Our heart surgeons use the latest technology to expertly diagnose and treat heart conditions, from intraoperative imaging technology in the operating room to the newest devices available for heart support.

Minimally invasive expertise:

We perform minimally invasive heart surgery whenever possible. This approach is an alternative to traditional, open-heart surgery, and leads to a faster recovery, shorter hospital stay, and a lower risk of complications, among other benefits.

Reputation:

Our heart surgery program at Cardiocare Clinic is known for its ground-breaking work in a number of heart procedures. We continue to lead the way with the development of new techniques – such as natural heart valve repair – for making even the most complex heart surgeries safer and more effective.
